## Context: PvP Gear and Tank Viability

In TBC, Resilience reduces the enemy crit chance against you at the same rate as Defense rating (~0.04% per point). This makes PvP gear genuinely useful for tanks who are below the 490 Defense cap, since Resilience can fill the gap. However, PvP gear has no Defense rating and lower Block Value — use it as a stepping stone, not a final solution.

**Crit immunity formula:** (Defense skill − 350) × 0.04% + Resilience rating × 0.04% ≥ 5.6%

- 2 pieces: +35 Resilience Rating (very impactful for crit immunity)
