## Context

Crafted items fall into two categories:
- **BoE** — Binds on Equip. Can be bought on the AH or commissioned from any craftsman. Best path for most players.
- **BoP / Profession-locked** — Requires the player themselves to have the profession (e.g., Armorsmithing). Cannot be bought — only crafted for personal use.

Plans/Patterns marked as **BoP** mean only a blacksmith who has that recipe can craft the item. The crafted item itself may still be BoE (sellable).

## Faith in Felsteel Set Bonus

Wearing Felsteel Helm + Felsteel Leggings + Felsteel Gloves activates:
- **3-piece bonus: +25 Strength** (adds threat, minor survivability benefit)

Not a primary reason to chase the set, but free if you already have all three pieces.
