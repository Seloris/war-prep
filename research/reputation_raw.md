## Gaps / Unverified

- **Scryers tank shoulder enchant**: Exact item name not confirmed. Community sources say +15 Defense / +10 Dodge, but item name and exact stats need Wowhead item page verification.
- **Glyph of the Defender rep level**: Sold by Alurmi (Keepers of Time), but exact reputation level (Honored vs Revered) was not cleanly confirmed from the page fetch.
- **Crest of the Sha'tar required rep**: Fetched as Exalted from item search; an earlier faction page summary said Honored. Wowhead item page shows Exalted and faction 935 — trust Exalted, but verify.
- **Ancient Draenei Crest exact stats**: Block value of 65 and armor of 2,938 come from a faction page summary, not a direct item page fetch. Verify on Wowhead.
- **Reservoir Key rep threshold**: Original TBC (pre-2.3) required Revered with Cenarion Expedition; patch 2.3 lowered it to Honored. TBC Anniversary Phase 1 patch state unclear — verify which applies.
- **Dauntless Handguards acquisition**: Listed as Honor Hold Honored on faction page, but item data references "Fel Embers" as transaction type. May be a quest-currency reward rather than a gold purchase from the quartermaster. Verify in-game or on Wowhead NPC page.
- **Maimfist's Choker**: Listed as Thrallmar Honored with +18 Def / +27 Sta / +17 Str. Could not verify on direct item page — a search for the item showed a raid-related source. Conflict unresolved; needs direct Wowhead item page check.
- **Alliance Honor Hold tank neck**: No comparable defense/stamina neck found for Alliance from Honor Hold. Slots with no Alliance alternative noted — may require dungeon loot or crafting.
- **Andormu's Tear**: Source listed as quest "Hero of the Brood" (KoT quest chain) not a rep vendor. Stats from search data snippet: +15 Block Rating, +10 Defense, +26 Dodge. Needs full item page verification.
- **Timelapse Shard use effect**: Only +27 Stamina passive is confirmed; the active/use effect was not captured.
- **Honor Hold head enchant**: No tank-focused head glyph found on the Honor Hold faction page. The defense head enchant (Glyph of the Defender) is from Keepers of Time. Verify that no additional head enchant exists from HH.
- **The Consortium**: Community notes this faction has poor rewards for warriors. No tank-relevant gear items were identified. No further research needed unless a specific item is referenced.
- **Cenarion Expedition gear**: No tank plate/shield items identified from CE beyond the Reservoir Key. The Glyph of Ferocity (+34 AP, +16 Hit) is a head enchant for DPS/melee — note hit rating makes it theoretically useful for threat, but a Protection Warrior does not use the Ferocity glyph (head slot would take Glyph of the Defender). No further CE tank gear expected.
- **Lower City beyond the shield**: No additional tank plate items confirmed from Lower City beyond the Ancient Draenei Crest (shield) and Auchenai Key. Verify if any ring, neck, or cloak with defense/stamina exists.
- **Sha'tar chest/shoulders/other slots**: Only the shield (Crest of the Sha'tar) and Warpforged Key were identified. Sha'tar Exalted also provides some plate items (Savage Plate Chestpiece with +46 Sta, +24 Crit from faction page) — these are crit/stamina without defense; may still be worth listing if no better option exists.
