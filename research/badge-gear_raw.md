## Context

Badges of Justice drop from every heroic dungeon boss (1 badge per boss) and later from raid bosses. G'eras is the vendor in Shattrath (Terrace of Light, second floor).
Badges are a bottlenecked resource — farm order matters. Prioritize the highest-impact items first.

**How to earn badges in Phase 1:** Heroic dungeon bosses only. ~5–7 bosses per heroic = 5–7 badges per run.

## Badge Priority Order for Phase 1

Badges are scarce in Phase 1 (heroics only). Suggested spend order based on impact-per-badge:

1. **Ring of Unyielding Force** — 25 badges. Cheap, immediate tank ring upgrade.
2. **Azure-Shield of Coldarra** — 33 badges. Best shield available pre-raid. Get this ASAP.
3. **Faceguard of Determination** — 50 badges. Strong head with meta socket.
4. **Necklace of the Juggernaut** — 75 badges. Best badge neck but expensive — farm heroics consistently.

Total for all Phase 1 tank items: **183 badges** (≈ 26–37 full heroic clears)
