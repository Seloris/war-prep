## Gaps / Unverified

- The Ultimate Bloodsport (Nagrand): Source spreadsheet listed this as rewarding a Warrior shield — **not confirmed by Wowhead**. All 6 reward options are ranged weapons and relics. This entry may have been confused with a different quest. Needs further verification.
- Clefthoof Hide Leggings and Flesh Handler's Gauntlets are leather — armor tradeoff vs. plate alternatives not yet quantified for the BiS list. Both are recommended pickups despite being off-spec armor.
- Shattrath Leggings vs. Clefthoof Hide Leggings: Both are leather legs at iLevel 109. Shattrath Leggings has Expertise +22 but no hit; Clefthoof has Hit +17 + Expertise +18. The choice depends on which cap you still need to reach.
- Overlord's Helmet of Second Sight has no defense stats — gemming 3 blue/yellow sockets is needed to keep above 490 defense if this is equipped.
- Scryer neck (Thalodien's Charm, iLevel 109) vs. Hound-Master tank neck (Mark of the Ravenguard, iLevel 115): Mark of the Ravenguard has higher iLevel and higher stamina but no block value. Thalodien's adds block value. Rank order needs BiS evaluation.
- The Flesh Lies quest ID not confirmed (item 30341 confirmed as quest reward from "The Flesh Lies" but quest ID not verified).
- Forge Camp: Annihilated iLevel discrepancy: search returned iLevel 103; confirm in-game or via tooltip.
- Dungeon quests listed in the original source spreadsheet (Mana-Tombs, Caverns of Time, Sethekk Halls, Steamvault, Shattered Halls) are NOT included here — they belong in dungeon-loot_raw.md.
