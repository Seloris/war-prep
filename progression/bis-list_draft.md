## Known Gaps (Research Incomplete)

- **Expertise:** No craftable/AH item provides expertise. Primary sources: leather group quest pieces (Clefthoof Hide Leggings, Flesh Handler's Gauntlets, Shattrath Leggings). Must equip leather to reach soft cap pre-heroic.
- **Hit cap path:** Breastplate of the Warbringer (+30 Hit) + Continuum Blade (+15 Hit) + socket contributions cover most of the 142 rating requirement. Verify exact total once all pieces are equipped.
- **Defense cap (490):** Felsteel Helm + Felsteel Leggings + Cloak of Eternity + Regal Protectorate + Glyph of the Defender (head enchant) provides substantial base defense without heroics. Exact total needs in-game verification.
- **Dungeon neck:** No dungeon neck drop identified. Mark of the Ravenguard (group quest) is the primary pre-raid neck.
- **Bracers of the Green Fortress:** Listed as wrist BiS but gated by Primal Nether (heroic only) — treat as Stage 3 acquisition despite being a craft.
