## Stage 1 — Day 1: Crafts & AH Purchases
*No prerequisites. Buy or commission these immediately. Answer to "what do I do today?"*

| Item | Slot | Source | Est. Cost | Priority | Notes |
|------|------|--------|-----------|----------|-------|
| Regal Protectorate | Trinket | Group quest — Cruel's Intentions (Hellfire Peninsula, quest ID 10136) | Solo/group quest, 0 gold | **#1 — Do first** | +26 Defense passive + HP use. Get this before your first dungeon run if below 490 Defense. Req level 58. |
| Felsteel Helm | Head | AH or Blacksmith commission (BoE) | ~200–400g (materials: 24 Felsteel Bars, 16 Eternium Bars) | **#2** | 3 sockets (1R/2B), +27 Stamina, +33 Defense. Best craftable head. Holds until Faceguard of Determination (50 badges). |
| Cloak of Eternity | Back | AH or Tailor commission (BoE) | ~50–150g | **#3** | +36 Stamina, +23 Defense. Holds until Devilshark Cape (Heroic Steamvault). Verify exact materials on Wowhead (item 24253). |
| Felsteel Leggings | Legs | AH or Blacksmith commission (BoE) | ~150–300g (materials: ~18 Felsteel Bars, 12 Eternium Bars) | **#4** | +39 Stamina, +33 Defense. Competitive enough to hold until Timewarden's Leggings (KoT Revered) or Greaves of the Shatterer (Heroic). Verify exact stats on Wowhead. |
| Felsteel Gloves | Hands | AH or Blacksmith commission (BoE) | ~80–150g (materials: ~10 Felsteel Bars, 6 Eternium Bars) | **#5** | +27 Stamina, +25 Defense, 2 sockets. Holds until Gauntlets of Dissension (Heroic Botanica). Part of Faith in Felsteel 3-piece (+25 Strength bonus with Helm + Legs). Verify exact stats on Wowhead. |
| Necklace of the Deep | Neck | AH or JC commission (BoE) | ~30–80g | **#6 — conditional** | +20 Stamina, +21 Agility, 2 Red sockets (+3 Hit socket bonus). Buy only if still under hit cap after Stage 2 quests (Breastplate of the Warbringer covers +30 Hit). Otherwise skip — Mark of the Ravenguard is better and free. |

**Stage 1 estimated gold:** ~500–1,100g total (varies by server economy)

**What Stage 1 does NOT include:**
- Bracers of the Green Fortress — craft, but requires Primal Nether from heroic dungeon → Stage 3
- Bulwark of Kings (chest) — BoP Armorsmithing, only if already an Armorsmith at 375
- Any item requiring rep, a dungeon key, or a group
