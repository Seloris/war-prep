## Stage 3 — Heroic Dungeon Farming
*Requires Honored (or Revered — see notes) with relevant factions for heroic keys.*
*Start with the easiest key and work outward. Collect badges from every heroic boss — they stack toward three purchases from G'eras.*

**Heroic key unlock order (fastest → slowest):**
1. Flamewrought Key — Honor Hold / Thrallmar Honored (fastest — Hellfire Peninsula quests + HFC normal runs)
2. Auchenai Key — Lower City Honored (quick via Auchindoun normal runs + Terokkar quests)
3. Reservoir Key — Cenarion Expedition Honored or Revered (verify TBC Anniversary patch — pre-2.3 required Revered; 2.3+ lowered to Honored)
4. Key of Time — Keepers of Time Honored (already farming KoT for Timewarden's Leggings — unlocks alongside)
5. Warpforged Key — The Sha'tar Revered (slowest — TK normal runs only, no quest rep; unlock last)

**Badge priority (spend in this order):** Ring of Unyielding Force (25 badges) → Azure-Shield of Coldarra (33 badges) → Faceguard of Determination (50 badges)

**Resilience note:** Resilience contributes to crit immunity at the same rate as Defense rating (~0.04% per point). Stage 4 pieces are especially useful if hitting 490 Defense cap is proving difficult — treat them as a Defense gap filler, not a permanent solution. Replace with plate once Defense cap is reached via enchants and heroic drops.
