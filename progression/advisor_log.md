## Q1 — 2026-03-21: What do I do with my money? Buy gear or level Blacksmithing first?

**Answer: Buy gear. Do not level Blacksmithing for the purpose of gear.**

### Reasoning

Every BiS craftable piece you need is BoE — meaning you can buy it off the AH or commission it from any craftsman:
- Felsteel Helm (Head) — BoE
- Felsteel Leggings (Legs) — BoE
- Felsteel Gloves (Hands) — BoE
- Bracers of the Green Fortress (Wrists) — BoE

You gain zero advantage from having Blacksmithing yourself for any of these. The gold you'd spend leveling BS (typically 200–400g to reach 375, plus opportunity cost) is better converted directly into gear.

The one exception is **Bulwark of Kings** (Chest, BiS-tier) — it is BoP Armorsmith-only. However:
1. It requires BS 375 + Armorsmithing specialization — a large investment.
2. It requires 10 Primal Nether, which drops from heroic dungeons (you need heroic access to farm them).
3. By the time you can realistically craft it, you will likely have a dungeon-drop chest or it won't be the bottleneck.
4. Its total material cost rivals spending that gold on other gear slots.

### Decision Rule
**Spend gold on BoE BiS pieces in priority order (see roadmap Stage 1).** Blacksmithing is a money sink, not a shortcut, unless you already have it leveled.

### Priority spend order (from craftable_raw.md data)
1. Felsteel Helm — best entry head slot, strong defense + sockets
2. Felsteel Leggings — best legs pre-raid (stamina + defense)
3. Bracers of the Green Fortress — best crafted wrists, Primal Nether is the bottleneck (heroic drop)
4. Felsteel Gloves — solid hands, also completes 3-piece set bonus (+25 Strength)
